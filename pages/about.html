About Guddoers Media — our vision & values

Welcome to our About Page!
We are a global media and storytelling platform based in Galway, Ireland, with a growing presence in Nigeria. We amplify voices, discover talent, and create positive impact through podcasts, interviews, documentaries, and digital storytelling.

We bring together inspiring people from all walks of life to share authentic stories, ideas, and experiences that educate, entertain, and empower audiences worldwide.


Our Mission
To amplify voices, discover hidden talent, and inspire positive change through authentic storytelling and high-quality media that informs, educates, and empowers communities.


Our Vision
To amplify voices, discover hidden talent, and inspire positive change through authentic storytelling and high-quality media that informs, educates, and empowers communities.


Our Core Values
Integrity • Excellence • Creativity • Empowerment • Community • Respect • Impact
These values guide everything we do and shape the stories we tell.


Our Story
Founded in 2024 by Tony Morris Agbor, Guddoers Media began as a podcast with a simple vision: to give ordinary people a platform to share extraordinary stories.

Today, we have grown into a media movement dedicated to discovering talent, celebrating communities, and inspiring positive change through impactful storytelling.
