The Studio

The Guddoers Podcast is a modern podcast studio located in Galway City on Ireland's beautiful west coast. We help individuals, creators, and businesses bring their ideas to life through high-quality interviews and engaging conversations across a wide range of topics, including lifestyle, security, entertainment, sports, business, and current affairs.


Studio Amenities: Accommodates up to 3 people, professionally soundproofed recording space, premium studio lighting, industry-grade cameras and microphones.
